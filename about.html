Prophet Odolo Joshua

The Man Behind the Ministry

Raised, Refined,
and Sent

Prophet Odolo Joshua is not a man who chose the pulpit — he is a man chosen by God from before his birth, shaped in the furnace of faith, and released in fire to this generation.

From an early age, Prophet Odolo Joshua encountered the living God in ways that set him apart. Marked by prophetic dreams, divine visitations, and an insatiable hunger for the Word, he understood from his youth that his life was not his own — it was consecrated to the purposes of heaven.

Called into full-time ministry after a profound encounter with the Holy Spirit, Prophet Odolo Joshua founded the Strong Hand of Jehovah Prophetic Ministry with one mandate: to be a house where the lost are found, the broken are made whole, the sick are healed, and the called are equipped.

His ministry is marked by accuracy in prophecy, demonstrable signs and wonders, and a fierce commitment to discipleship. Under his leadership, SHJ has become a spiritual family that reaches not only Lagos but nations — through online broadcasts, prophetic gatherings, and anointed resources that carry the tangible presence of God.

Prophet Odolo Joshua moves in the fivefold office of the prophet — declaring the word of the Lord with boldness, interceding with intensity, and equipping believers to walk in supernatural authority over every area of their lives. He carries a special burden for the poor, the overlooked, and the broken, operating from the conviction that God's power is most glorified through human impossibility.

His motto — "Through Faith and Belief" — is not a tagline. It is the bedrock of every prayer prayed, every sermon preached, and every miracle manifested under this ministry.

The Journey of a Prophet

Milestones of
This Ministry

The Beginning
A Divine Encounter

Prophet Odolo Joshua receives a life-altering encounter with the Holy Spirit — a moment that would define the rest of his days. He surrenders fully to the call of God and begins to move in the prophetic gifts that had quietly stirred within him from childhood.

The Mandate
Strong Hand of Jehovah Is Founded

Driven by a clear mandate from God, Prophet Odolo Joshua establishes the Strong Hand of Jehovah Prophetic Ministry. What begins as a small gathering of hungry believers quickly grows into a powerful spiritual community marked by signs, wonders, and radical life transformations.

The Expansion
A House of Encounter Is Built

The ministry grows beyond expectations. New converts flood the doors, testimonies of healing and deliverance spread through communities, and SHJ becomes known as a place where the presence of God is tangible and the miraculous is normal.

The Vision
Building God's Permanent House

Prophet Odolo Joshua receives a clear vision from the Lord: a permanent sanctuary — a physical altar and centre of operations that will serve this generation and the next. The building project is launched, funded by faithful partners who believe in this God-given vision.

Today & Beyond
Nations, Not Just Neighbourhoods

The ministry has extended its reach through online broadcasts, anointed resources, and prophetic gatherings that touch lives across Nigeria and beyond. The best chapters of this story are still being written — and you are a part of them.
