Throughout the Year

Special Services
& Events

Beyond the weekly calendar, SHJ hosts powerful gatherings that mark seasons of breakthrough.

Monthly — Last Friday
All-Night Vigil
10:00 PM – 4:00 AM

A night of intense intercession, prophetic worship, and breakthrough prayer. Many testimonies of divine turnarounds have emerged from these vigils. Come hungry, leave transformed.

Quarterly
Prophetic Summit
Friday – Sunday

A three-day intensive gathering focused on hearing God, receiving prophetic activation, and understanding your season. Guest prophets and ministers alongside our leadership.

Annual — January
21 Days of Prayer & Fasting
6:00 AM Daily

We open every year with 21 days of corporate fasting and prayer. This is the spiritual foundation that carries the ministry through the year. Prayer guides distributed to all members.

Annual — December
Crossover Night
10:00 PM – Midnight

End the year in the presence of God and cross into the New Year with prophetic declarations and supernatural positioning. One of the most anticipated nights of the SHJ calendar.

Bi-Annual
Healing & Miracle Service
Saturday, 10:00 AM

Dedicated entirely to healing ministry. The anointing for healing flows powerfully in these services. Bring the sick — bring those who need a miracle. Nothing is impossible with God.

Monthly
New Members Induction
Sunday, After 2nd Service

A warm welcome and foundational orientation for all new members of the SHJ family. Includes connection with leadership, ministry discovery, and covenant membership teaching.

Have You Met
Jesus Christ?

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life." — John 3:16

01
Admit

Acknowledge that you are a sinner and that you cannot save yourself. "For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." — Romans 3:23

02
Believe

Believe that Jesus Christ died for your sins and rose from the dead. He is the only way, the truth, and the life. — John 14:6

03
Confess

Pray This Prayer Out Loud

Lord Jesus, I come before you today as a sinner. I believe that you died on the cross for my sins and rose again on the third day. I repent of all my sins and I ask you to forgive me. Come into my heart, Lord Jesus. I confess you as my Lord and my Saviour. From this day forward, I belong to you. Fill me with your Holy Spirit and help me to live for you. In Jesus' name. Amen.
